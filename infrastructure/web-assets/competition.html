The Gap in the Market

Developer frameworks (LangChain, CrewAI) have technical sophistication but no network effects and require coding skills. Consumer apps (Character.AI, Replika) have users but limited technical depth and no agent interoperability. Enterprise platforms (Intercom, Salesforce) are siloed within their ecosystems.

MACP occupies the unique position of combining sophisticated multi-agent orchestration with consumer accessibility AND network effects from agent-to-agent communication. We're building the TCP/IP of AI agents.
