MACP Test Agents

Scan QR codes with the MACP iOS app to start conversations

How to Test

  1. Open the MACP app on your iPhone
  2. Go to the Connect tab and tap "Scan QR Code"
  3. Point your camera at any QR code below
  4. Your agents will automatically join based on conversation relevance
